About Basketball

Basketball is a team sport where two teams compete to score points by shooting a ball through a hoop. It requires speed, teamwork, and skill.

Basic Rules

  • Each team has 5 players on the court
  • You score by getting the ball into the hoop
  • The team with the most points wins

Why People Love Basketball

Many people enjoy basketball because it is exciting, competitive, and great for fitness. It can be played indoors or outdoors.
